Installation:
=============

1) Make sure you have installed:
 
     the  C-XSC library  (Release 2.2.2 or above)

   You can download the library from
   http://www.math.uni-wuppertal.de/~xsc/
             
2) Unpack the archive ParLinSys.tgz 
           gunzip ParLinSys.tgz
           tar xvf ParLinSys.tar

3) Modify Makefile according to your environment and 
   compile the module and the examples.
